Learn Spanish through our full immersion program. Forget about books and grammar lessons. Conversation is key. Actively learn Spanish while living like a local.

Experience

Experience all Granada has to offer while spending your days with native Spanish speakers. Learn about the history and culture of this magnificent city and discover the secrets hidden away from tourists.

16 hours of Spanish a day

We can guarantee you total immersion for 16 hours a day. Think about it, that's the amount of hours you would cover in two months if you go two classes a week. This is the best and fastest way to learn any language.

Language



Our immersive language program is about creating a supportive community to optimize language learning, where every moment becomes an opportunity for language exploration. The nature of the program is all about conversation, designed to depart from book study and grammar lessons to actively use Spanish in a variety of contexts. Student participants will be exposed to a full spectrum of the language, in an intimate and relaxing setting.

Culture



Our unique cultural excursions give students experiences only locals normally have access to. Unlike a typical trip where you need to rely on guided tours, we provide direct contact with the culture of Andalucia. Whether it be through dancing flamenco in the caves of Sacromonte, or eating Jamon Serrano in Spain's highest village, students and locals find commonalities through shared experiences.

Adventure



Many of our programs offer adventure activities that push participants outside of their comfort zones in a safe and supportive environment. We want to challenge participants to try something new, to have fun, and to learn what they are capable of doing. Trying new things is a part of every adventure! However, adventure activities are always optional and our staff members can help offer alternative options should a student not wish to participate.
