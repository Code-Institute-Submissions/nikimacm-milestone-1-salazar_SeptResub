Salazar Language and Cultural Holidays.

Learn Spanish through our full immersion program. Forget about books and grammer lessons. Conversation is key. Actively learn Spanish while living like a local.

Experience

Experience all Granada has to offer while spending your days with native Spanish speakers. Learn about the history and culture of this magnificent city and discover the secrets hidden away from tourists.

16 hours of Spanish a day

We can guarantee you total immersion for 16 hours a day. Think about it, that's the amount of hours you would cover in two months if you go two classes a week. This is the best and fastest way to learn any language.

learn outdoors surrounded by nature

Whether walking in the Alpuharras, horseriding, skiing or simply enjoying our conversation hours in the garden, you will definitely feel the benifits of outdoor learning. A concept which we here at Salazar strongly believe in.
